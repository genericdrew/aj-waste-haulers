## 1. Required Pages (MUST)

| **Services Index** | 5/5 | Submenu only | MUST | Competence; a navigable index page with cards linking to individual service detail pages is the standard. AJ's nav submenu generates no SEO value and provides no trust depth per service. |

| **FAQ** | 0/5 standalone (2/5 on homepage) | Nav link exists (unpopulated) | MUST | Character + SEO; no competitor has a standalone FAQ page — AJ already has the nav link. An FAQPage schema implementation here is a first-mover advantage. AJ nav currently links to an empty or thin page. |
| **Service Area Hub** | 3/5 | Not built | MUST | Competence + Local SEO; AJ lists 5 cities as prose; 3 competitors have hub pages linking to individual city pages. Must exist before individual city pages can be navigated. |

## 5. Pages NOT to Build (v1)

Page types competitors have — or AJ has a nav link for — that do not serve AJ at this stage.

| Page Type | Competitor Prevalence | Reason NOT to Build in v1 |
|---|:---:|---|
| **Blog** | 1/5 (Superior Septic C3 only; ~monthly cadence) | AJ has a Blog nav link already; only 1 competitor has an active blog. A blog without a documented content plan and committed publication cadence generates no SEO value and creates a "last updated 2 years ago" credibility liability. **Decision: NOT YET.** Add when client commits to ≥1 post/month with an assigned author. The Drought + Your Septic page and the Grease Trap Compliance Guide (differentiation pages above) should be built first — they are evergreen and require no ongoing editorial commitment. |
| **Resources / Educational Hub** | 1/5 (Aloha Septic C2 "Septic Basics" page) | AJ has a Resources nav link already with no confirmed content behind it. A resources hub requires substantial content investment to outperform a single competitor page. **Decision: NOT YET for the hub.** The individual differentiation pages (drought, compliance, what-to-expect, pricing) are higher-value standalone assets that collectively achieve the same function without the overhead of a hub index. Consolidate to a hub in v2 when ≥4 educational articles exist. |
| **Team / Staff Directory** | 1/5 (Aloha Septic C2 only) | Only one competitor has a standalone team page; AJ's team is two founders. The team content (photos, bios, credentials, founding story) belongs on the About page, not as a separate page. A separate team page for 2 people is redundant and dilutes E-E-A-T authority to two URLs. **Decision: NOT NEEDED — integrate into About page.** |
| **Portable Toilet Rentals** | 2/5 (Superior C3, Action C5) | AJ's intake document does not list portable toilet rentals as a service. **Decision: NOT APPLICABLE — service not offered.** |
| **Dewatering** | 1/5 (Action Septic C5 only) | Not listed in AJ's service inventory. **Decision: NOT APPLICABLE — service not offered.** |
| **Car Wash Pit Cleaning** | 2/5 (Superior C3, A+ C4) | Not listed in AJ's service inventory. **Decision: NOT APPLICABLE — service not offered.** |
| **Aerobic System Maintenance Contracts** | 2/5 (Hill Country C1, Aloha C2) | AJ's intake does not list aerobic maintenance contracts as a service. **Decision: NOT APPLICABLE unless client confirms this service.** If confirmed, add as a 6th service detail page. |
| **Sitemap (HTML)** | 1/5 (A+ C4 only) | XML sitemap is infrastructure and will be auto-generated by Hugo. An HTML sitemap page adds no conversion value and no SEO value above the XML version. **Decision: NOT NEEDED — generate XML only.** |
| **Privacy Policy / Terms of Use** | 1/5 (A+ C4 only) | Legal pages are required if AJ collects personal data via forms. **Decision: DEFER — build as a plain text page in v2 when form infrastructure is in place; not a launch blocker unless form goes live in v1.** |
| **Pricing (specific dollar amounts)** | 0/5 | The differentiation pricing page above ("What Septic Pumping Costs in Central Texas") is educational, not a rate card. Publishing specific prices without a client-approved price list creates customer service friction and may not reflect actual pricing. **Decision: Build the educational pricing page (§3 above) but do NOT publish a rate card until the client approves specific prices. The page explains factors that affect cost and directs to a free quote CTA.** |
