## 6. Top 5 Tactical Takeaways for the AJ Rebuild

**Takeaway 1 — Deploy city landing pages immediately for your 5 core cities**
A+ Septic ranks in Dripping Springs with a dedicated page listing 30+ neighborhood names. AJ's home turf is Dripping Springs yet has zero local SEO infrastructure for it. Create individual pages for: Dripping Springs, Austin, Driftwood, Kyle, New Braunfels — each with localized intro text, local landmarks, and zip codes. This is the single highest-ROI SEO move given AJ's geographic focus.

**Takeaway 2 — Publish on-site testimonials on the homepage; add a Reviews page**
4 of 5 competitors embed real customer names and quotes on the homepage and/or a dedicated reviews page. AJ sends visitors to Google with no social proof in sight. Pull 8–12 Google reviews onto the site and structure them with `schema.org/Review` markup. Even 3 visible quotes above the fold will outperform the current "Write a Review" link.

**Takeaway 3 — Surface the beverage-industry niche as a standalone service page**
No competitor markets winery/brewery/distillery waste removal. Dripping Springs brands itself "Distillery Capital of Texas" and has a dense cluster of Hill Country wineries, breweries, and distilleries within AJ's service radius. A targeted service page with relevant keywords ("winery waste removal Dripping Springs," "brewery liquid waste Austin") can own this entirely.

**Takeaway 4 — Create an About/Team page with founder photos and named experience claims**
Aloha Septic's About page (founder Chris Osburn, wife Monica, son Keoni) generates trust that an anonymous company page cannot. AJ's founders have 40+ combined years — this claim sits in a hidden intake doc, not the website. A family-story About page with real photos and the "proof-of-pumpage" technology explanation is a high E-E-A-T move that directly differentiates from fly-by-night competition.

**Takeaway 5 — Add a sticky phone number + embedded quote form to the homepage**
Every single competitor places a prominent phone CTA in the header and/or a service-request form above the fold. AJ has "Contact Us" in the nav. A form or "Get a Free Quote" button integrated into the hero section, plus a sticky phone bar on mobile, directly captures more leads from visitors who already want to hire — no copy needed to close them.
